About Dropay

Dropay is a modern, secure, and easy-to-use merchant payment platform designed to empower businesses of all sizes to accept payments seamlessly across Nigeria. Whether you're a small business owner, a freelancer, or a growing enterprise, Dropay gives you the tools to receive customer payments quickly and reliably without the usual technical headaches.
At the core of Dropay is simplicity and speed. We eliminate the barriers that often come with traditional payment systems by offering a streamlined onboarding process, instant access to your merchant dashboard, and real-time transaction tracking. With Dropay, you can easily generate payment links, receive alerts on every payment, and manage all your earnings in one place.
